Vegetarian In The Bay Area

歡迎大家來到這個蔬適空間！

澳洲大火、新型冠狀病毒、東非蝗災、非洲豬瘟...好多生命因此受傷喪命，讓我們一起為地球祈福，也為自己祈福，用蔬食為地球加油打氣！實踐低碳生活，為減緩氣候變遷，降低溫室氣體排放，我們可以選擇低碳排的生活方式：少肉多蔬、食用當季在地食材、多搭乘大眾交通工具、使用省電型家電、支持再生能源發展等。讓我們一起用行動表示對生命的關愛!

想要藉由這個平台來分享在灣區的蔬食餐廳，或是蔬食煮食的創意食譜。我們歡迎各種素，凡舉奶蛋素、全素，五辛素、健康素都可以，只要非肉類的美食，都歡迎向我們分享資訊唷。


Welcome to Vegetarian In The Bay Area!

Australia's bushfires, the COVID-19, the locust plague in East Africa, the African swine fever virus. A lot of lives were injured and killed. Let us pray for the earth together. To practice low-carbon life, to slow down climate change and reduce greenhouse gas emissions, we can choose a low-carbon lifestyle, for example, less meat and more vegetables, eating local ingredients in the season, taking more public transportation, using power-saving appliances, and supporting Renewable energy development, etc.. Let us use our actions to express our love for life!

This platform shares vegetarian restaurants in the Bay Area and creative veggie recipes. We welcome all kinds of vegetarians, including vegan, Lacto-Ovo vegetarian, and healthy vegetarian, as long as it is non-meat food, you are welcome to share information with us.
