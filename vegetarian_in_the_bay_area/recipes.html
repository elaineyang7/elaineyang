Gold Ingot Dumpling

An easy homemade vegetarian dumplings recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 Baby Bok Choy
  • 2 Wood Ear
  • 1 Cellophane Noodles
  • 1 block Braised Tofu
  • 3 tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 1 tbsp Sesame Oil
  • 1 pinch White Pepper
  • 1 pinch Salt

Instructions

  1. Chop baby bok choy, wood ear, rice noodles, and tofu into small chunks
  2. Put all the filling ingredients in a large bowl and mix until combined.
  3. Place a tablepoon of filling in the middle of the wrapper.
  4. Fold the wrapper in half.
  5. Wet the bottom left corner of the wrapper. Hold both corners of the wrapper and fold them to the center.
