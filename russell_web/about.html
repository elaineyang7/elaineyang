About

Russell Leong Design

The prevailing attitude at Russell Leong Design is that design should reflect one’s life. For Russell Leong this means taking a serious approach and introducing aspects of humanity and humor when appropriate. This attitude along with the tempering of personal style, function and sensitivity to client needs are essential factors in the continuing success of Russell Leong Design.

Located in the heart of Silicon Valley

Clients

Leong has kept his office small by design, yet maintains a large range of clients which varies in size from small retail to large Fortune 500 Silicon Valley firms. Just to name a few below.

In addition, Leong has done some non-profit projects.

Others

Media

RLD's work has been published in design annuals produced by the San Francisco Art Directors Club, Western Art Directors Club, Art Directors Club of Los Angeles, New York Art Directors Club, Houston Art Directors Club and Print Magazine Regional Annual, and the American Institute of Graphic Arts (AIGA).


Work has also appeared in periodicals such as: Communication Arts, STEP, Dynamic Graphics, HOW, Novum, and Idea Magazines.

Collection

RLD also has work represented in the US Library of Congress permanent collection.

Teaching

Leong's extensive background includes teaching and lecturing at California State University/San Jose, University of California Santa Cruz Extension, and Academy of Art University.
